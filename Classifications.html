Classification Of Substances

Substances are basic forms of matter with specific properties, consisting of one type of atom or a combination of different atoms bonded together. They can exist as elements or compounds.

Separation of Immiscible Solid Liquid Mixtures

Decantation

Decantation is the process of separation of liquid from solid and other immiscible (non-mixing) liquids, by removing the liquid layer at the top from the layer of solid or liquid below.

The process can be carried out by tilting the mixture after pouring out the top layer.

Examples of mixtures that can be separated through decantation: sand and water,maize grains and water,etc

Filtration

Filtration-A filter paper and a funnel can be uses to separate a mixture of sand and water,fine sand particle will remain on the filter paper and are known as residue while the water will pass through the filter paper and will be called filtrate
